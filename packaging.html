My Process

01

Ideation · Concept

Explore creative directions, define goals, and establish a clear packaging story aligned to the brand.

02

Refinement

Iterate dielines, visual systems, and copy. Tighten art direction and prepare production-ready layouts.

03

Licensor Approval

Collaborate with stakeholders and licensors to finalize artwork and receive formal approvals.

04

Production · Factory Handoff

Deliver mechanicals, specs, and assets to vendors; support prepress and ensure accurate manufacturing.
